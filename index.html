CDC MACHINE (Cattle dung collector)

  1. Importance of Efficient Cow Dung Management

    • Cow dung is a valuable organic resource in agricultural and livestock farming
    • Efficient management of cow dung is crucial for maximizing its potential benefit

  2. Introduction to Cow Dung Collector:

    • We present you the design and development of a cow dung collector
    • The collector is a novel solution aimed at optimizing the dung collection proces

  3. Objectives of the Dung Collector:

    • To minimize manual labor involved in dung collection.
    • To improve overall efficiency in cow dung management.

  4. Working Mechanism:

    • The dung collector integrates a mechanical rotor that pushes the dung towards collecting tank.
    • It also incorporates a pressurized water jet system to clean the surface spraying water at high velocity

  5. Benefits of the Dung Collector:

    • Minimizes the need for manual labour in the collection process.
    • Optimizes the efficiency of cow dung collection and management Reduces the time and effort required for dung collection.
    • Reduces overall cost

  6. Collection and Emptying Process:

    • The collected dung is stored in a tank
    • When the tank is full, a person can manually slide it out for emptying.

  7. Environmental Impact:

    • The dung collector promotes sustainable agricultural practices by utilizing cow dung as an organic resource.
    • It reduces the environmental impact associated with manual dung collection and disposal

  8. Conclusion:

    • The design and development of the cow dung collector offer an innovative solution for efficient dung management in agricultural and livestock farming practices.
    • By automating the collection process and minimizing manual labor, the collector optimizes the utilization of cow dung as a valuable organic resource.
